Maldives

Maldives is popular among tourists for its naturally pleasant environment including the blue ocean, white beaches and clean air. The weather in Maldives is also appropriate for various water sports and activities. Maldives is ranked among the best recreational diving destinations of the world, with over 60 local dive sites across the islands.
